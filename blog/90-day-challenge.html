The 90-Day Challenge: From 0 to 18,000 Leads

MLGS is not a "sprint." It is a snowball. Most people quit after 14 days because they don't see the vision. Here is what the math looks like if you stay consistent:

Day 1: 100-200 Leads (The Seed)

Day 30: 3,000-6,000 Leads (The Foundation)

Day 90: 9,000-18,000 Leads (The Breakthrough)

When you are mailing 18,000 people every morning, your chances of making a sale aren't just "better"—they are inevitable. This is a game of probability. The only way to lose is to stop playing.

The Goal

Commit to mailing once a day for 90 days. Don't look at the sales. Look at the lead count. When you hit 10k leads, the magic starts to happen.
